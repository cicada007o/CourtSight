About Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023

The Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) 2023 is India's new comprehensive criminal code that replaced the colonial-era Indian Penal Code (IPC) 1860.

This modernized legal framework reflects contemporary Indian values and addresses current social realities while maintaining the fundamental principles of justice. The BNS introduces new offences, updates existing definitions, and streamlines legal procedures for better access to justice.
